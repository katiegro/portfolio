Content

Home page

• Why will people come to your site? People will come to my site to visually see my photography portfolio.They can get an idea of the style and types of pictures I take. People can visit the site in order to book a shoot with me. Potential clients can read about me as a photographer and view my style of photography. They can see prices and choose to schedule an appointment with me. • What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? o Some questions that visitors will have answered when they come to my site are the style of photos I take. Also, the pictures I’ve previously taken will be displayed in order to help them see the type of products they’ll get if they choose to book with me. Another question that the site will answer is what my prices are and rates. • What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers? o Some necessary links are links to my personal as well as business Instagram accounts. Through these links potential clients can get an overview of me as a photographer and how I display my work online. Potential clients can get Ideas of a location they prefer for their shoot as well as pose ideas and even props. • Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions? o Visitors will come to my site as opposed to others because I have a well put together portfolio that showcases all the types of pictures I’ve taken as well as subjects I’ve used. I will display a versatile portfolio that has something to offer for everyone. I will also have affordable pricing and rates.

Image Gallery

• Image gallery o The image gallery will showcase the different skills I know how to utilize. Some of the skills are, depth of field, portraits, black and white, motion, ect. By having an image gallery on my site potential clients can see my work and evaluate whether they want to book an appointment with me. An image gallery can also make it easier for visitors to share specific images on social media platforms. This can potentially increase the reach and virality of the websites content, possibly attract ting ore visitors and generating greater exposure.

Images for the Page 2

FAQ

• What different tools will the website offer to visitors? o The website will offer many useful tools such as an image gallery, listed pricing, frequently asked questions, and a get to know me page. The image gallery will showcase the many types of photos I’ve taken so that potential clients grasp my personal style of photography. An image gallery can also provide clients with ideas on where they might want to have their shoot as well as pose ideas. Clients can get inspiration to help them better understand what they want so I, as the photographer, can determine the right direction of the shoot. Listed pricing is another tool that my website will offer in order to help potential clients understand the prices I offer and find something within their budget. Because I am not a professional my prices are more affordable than many photographers. Frequently asked questions will be available in order to help confused visitors and reassure them with the answers they’re looking for. Lastly, the get to know me page will help clients feel more comfortable before they even meet me. The get to know me page offers information about me such as schooling, hobbies, and interest in photography. Hopefully clients will feel more comfortable knowing a little bit about the person taking their pictures.
